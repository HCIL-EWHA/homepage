Welcome! The Human-Computer Interaction Lab (HCIL) at Ewha (HCIL@EWHA) founded by 2020 by Assistant Professor Uran Oh in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Ewha Womans University. Our lab's mission is to improve information access by augmenting or complementing users' physical or cognitive capabilities with state-of-art interaction technologies (e.g., wearables, extended reality).
